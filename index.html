// Who We Are

BUILT FOR
CHAMPIONS

DC Ultimate Frisbee Academy was founded with a singular purpose: to grow Ultimate Frisbee in Malaysia by giving the next generation of players a structured, high-level environment to develop and compete.

We specialise in training high school students — bridging the gap between casual play and competitive excellence. Our coaching team brings deep knowledge of the game and a passion for developing not just athletes, but leaders who embody the Spirit of the Game.

Our academy is a recognised pipeline into the Malaysian U20 National Team, providing the training environment, tactical depth, and competitive exposure needed to represent the nation.

Our Mission
To develop the next generation of Malaysian Ultimate players through elite coaching, school partnerships, and a culture built on Spirit of the Game.
