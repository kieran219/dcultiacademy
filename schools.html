// Why Ultimate Frisbee?

A SPORT BUILT
DIFFERENTLY

Ultimate Frisbee is one of the only sports in the world with no referee. Players govern themselves. Every call, every decision, every moment of accountability — players own it.

That unique property makes Ultimate Frisbee a natural environment for developing leadership, communication and integrity. We don't ask schools to believe in frisbee. We ask them to give their students one session to try it.

DC Ultimate Academy delivers a turnkey CCA programme — we handle coaching, curriculum and session planning. Minimal workload for your staff. Maximum development for your students.
