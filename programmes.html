// Session Structure

A TYPICAL
SESSION

Every 50–60 minute session follows a progressive structure — building fitness, technical skill and game intelligence simultaneously.

01
Dynamic Warm-Up & Movement Prep
Sport-specific warm-up activating the hips, shoulders and footwork patterns used in Ultimate Frisbee.
02
Technical Skill Development
Throwing mechanics, cutting patterns, defensive positioning — broken down and drilled progressively.
03
Game-Based Drills & Tactical Application
Skills applied in game-like scenarios — developing decision-making, communication and team coordination.
04
Small-Sided Gameplay
Competitive play in smaller formats — fast-paced, high-touch and self-officiated to build game intelligence.
05
Reflection & Debrief
Short team reflection on what was learned — reinforcing Spirit of the Game principles and leadership habits.
